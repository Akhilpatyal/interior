    The scope of each project will be clearly defined in the quotation and work order shared with the client before commencement.

    Project initiation requires an advance payment, with subsequent installments tied to specific project milestones.

    • Modular Kitchens: ~30 days
    • Wardrobes: ~20 days
    • Standard Packages/Remodels: ~30 days

    Timelines may vary depending on design complexity, material availability, and site conditions.


  4. Warranty Policy

    We stand behind the quality of our work.

    • Standard Warranty: All modular kitchens, wardrobes, and interior packages include a 10-year warranty on materials and craftsmanship.

      • Bathroom vanities and similar units carry a 1-year warranty.
      • Natural variations in wood tone, grain, or minor changes over time are not considered defects.
    • What’s Not Covered:
      • Damage due to misuse, negligence, improper cleaning, or repairs/alterations carried out by unauthorised personnel.
      • Normal wear and tear, exposure to extreme weather, moisture damage, or pest infestations.
