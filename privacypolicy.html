At Garrison Grove Interiors, we value your trust. Protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it is central to how we do business. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use, share, and safeguard it.


  1. Information We Collect

    When you interact with our website or fill out our consultation form, we may collect:

    • Personal Information:
      Name, email address, phone number, location, property type (villa, apartment, independent house), and your design requirements.
    • System Information:
      Browser type, IP address, and other technical details automatically collected when you visit our site.
    • Communication Data:
      Records of any emails, calls, or WhatsApp messages exchanged with our team.

  2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your details strictly for the purposes outlined below:

    • To contact you regarding your inquiry, consultation, or project.
    • To provide quotes, design proposals, and service updates.
    • To coordinate site visits, deliveries, and installations.
    • To send you occasional updates about our services, new offers, or events (you can unsubscribe at any time).
    • To comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.
